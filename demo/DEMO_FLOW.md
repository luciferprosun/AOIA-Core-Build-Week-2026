# Planned bounded demo flow

> **IN PROGRESS — NOT FINAL SUBMISSION EVIDENCE**

1. A human opens a lightweight local demonstration.
2. The human selects or enters a bounded informational query.
3. The app displays source/provenance and explicit non-authority labels.
4. A safe UNIX example may show deterministic retrieval/review metadata.
5. Any action-like request is shown as blocked or as a non-executing preview.
6. The human remains the decision-maker and closes the flow without autonomous execution.

German Law and Web Engineering content may be added only after separate audit, licensing, and human approval. This flow is a design target, not a readiness claim.
